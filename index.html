Revolutionizing Payments for Underserved Industries

At Tangi, we provide banking and payment processing solutions to industries that have been overlooked by traditional financial institutions, and we’ve specifically developed a solution for the cannabis industry—a sector that faces massive challenges in accessing banking and payment services.

Cannabis Payment Revolution

In the cannabis industry, banks refuse to service the market, and payment providers are left to operate in the shadows or outside the law. This has created a $33 billion cash-only market, where 8,000 retailers and 60 million consumers are forced to deal with security risks, inefficiencies, and a lack of payment options that are standard in every other retail sector. We’ve solved this problem.


Tangi is the first company to secure a compliant banking partner, enabling us to offer a transparent, legal payment solution that integrates traditional card payments alongside the Tangi token. This achievement is monumental—while others have failed, we’ve created the first legitimate, bank-backed payment solution for the cannabis industry.

Tangi Token: The Core of Our Payment Ecosystem

Now, here’s where the Tangi token comes in: the token is at the core of our solution. We’ve designed the entire system around it, making it the most rewarding payment option for retailers and consumers. Here’s how it works: We first capture customers through our trusted card payment services, leveraging familiarity and convenience. Once they’re onboard, we seamlessly transition them to using the Tangi token, which offers lower fees, rewards, and exclusive incentives. This is not just about the token; it’s the backbone of a fully integrated payment ecosystem.
