Our Story


Tangi has been in development for 5 years, and we are incredibly proud of what we've achieved. From the early days in 2021, we have had a committed and supportive community that has helped us shape our journey. What started as a crypto-only payment solution for the cannabis industry has since evolved into something far more impactful. Our earliest supporters have been with us through the journey, and their patience has been instrumental in getting us to where we are today.


Why We Pivoted

When we first began, we had a clear vision of publicly listing the token in 2022, and we intended to launch as a purely crypto-based payment solution. However, we quickly realized that in order to serve the cannabis industry effectively and sustainably, we needed to navigate a far more complex problem: access to banking services. As you may know, the legal cannabis industry is locked out of traditional banking services, leaving retailers and consumers reliant on cash-only transactions. In 2024 alone, consumers spent $33 billion in cash at over 8,000 retail stores. This has created an enormous, underserved market, and the real challenge for any payment provider in this space is securing a banking partner.

We were on the cusp of launching our original payment solution when we were presented with a groundbreaking opportunity: to partner with a bank and develop the first-ever legally compliant card payment solution for the cannabis industry. This was a pivotal moment in our journey. The decision to pivot was difficult; it meant delays and potential frustration for some of our early investors. But as our CEO Ron Strauss explains, “We knew we couldn’t pass up this opportunity. It wasn’t just about launching a token—it was about changing the entire landscape of cannabis payments.”

The Revolution in Cannabis Payments

What emerged from this strategic pivot is nothing short of revolutionary: a dual payment system that offers both traditional bank-integrated card payments and a highly rewarding token payment option, all operating seamlessly from the same terminal. This wasn’t just a detour; it became a game-changing move that positioned Tangi as the first compliant payment solution for the cannabis industry, a market that has long been underserved due to regulatory and banking barriers.

Our first-mover advantage comes from our ability to offer a compliant, transparent, and reliable payment service, coupled with the flexibility of both traditional and token payments. While the card solution serves as a means to capture consumers, the token—the core driver of our platform—offers consumers and retailers unparalleled rewards, cost savings, and future-proofed payments. With the banking relationship secured, we now have all the pieces in place to dominate the market.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike many other providers who have failed to secure the necessary banking support or offer viable solutions, Tangi is built on a rock-solid foundation: the first legal payment solution for cannabis retailers, powered by a banking partner that has committed to working with us exclusively. While our competitors continue to struggle with regulatory compliance and face the constant threat of their operations being shut down, we have the security of knowing we are not just compliant, but positioned to lead the market. We have created a scalable, fully compliant infrastructure that ensures retailers and consumers alike can trust the payment system we provide.

The significance of securing a banking relationship cannot be overstated. This is the missing piece that has kept the market fragmented, and we are the first to have solved this problem. The 8,000+ cannabis retailers that need a compliant solution can now rely on Tangi for their payment needs, a major breakthrough for the industry.

Market Expansion and Adoption

We have already successfully tested our solution in real-world conditions, processing $24 million in transactions over a 9-month period in collaboration with one of the industry’s largest retail players. However, a difference in vision led us to part ways with our partners, who have since gone out of business due to their inability to meet compliance standards. This only strengthened our resolve to take control of our own technology and develop an even more comprehensive solution.

Our strategy is now focused on rapid adoption. We plan to roll out our compliant payment services in an increasing number of retail locations, building on our early success to quickly capture market share. With our secure banking partnership and dual payment solution, the road to mass adoption is clear. By offering traditional card payment services alongside our highly rewarding token payment option, we are ensuring that we attract consumers from all angles, driving adoption and loyalty.

A New Standard for Investment

Tangi is not just another crypto project; it’s a real-world solution that solves a pressing need in a market worth $33 billion. Unlike other tokens that rely on hype and speculation, Tangi is backed by real-world adoption and genuine utility. Our partnership with the bank and our focus on compliance means that we are on track to capture a substantial portion of this market.

By investing in Tangi, you are backing a first-mover advantage in a market that is ready for disruption. We have already laid the groundwork, and as our token gains traction, its value will be driven by its utility, not just speculation. Our investors, who have supported us through thick and thin, are poised to benefit as we continue our growth and eventual public listing.

This is a company built on integrity, transparency, and a clear vision for success. We invite you to be part of our journey as we continue to lead the charge in revolutionizing cannabis payments and creating lasting value for our investors and the entire industry.
