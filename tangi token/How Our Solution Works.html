How Our Solution Works

How Our Solution Works: From First Use to Regular Token Payments


At Tangi, we’ve designed a seamless, transparent, and rewarding payment solution that not only addresses the cannabis industry’s need for compliant payment methods but also integrates our token into the process from the very beginning. Here’s how it works step by step:


1. Capturing the Consumer


Our journey starts by introducing consumers to our payment solution through traditional, trusted payment options. This includes familiar methods such as debit cards, credit cards, and tap-and-go payments—methods consumers already use daily in other industries. This familiarity provides the comfort and security they’ve been craving and makes the transition to our payment terminals smooth and natural.

Because cannabis consumers have been waiting for a legal, transparent, and compliant solution for years, we expect them to gravitate toward our payment options without hesitation. No aggressive marketing is required—these consumers want and need this service, and it will be readily available for them when they make their purchases at participating retailers.


2. Introducing the Token Payment Option


Once consumers have used our traditional payment methods, we begin to introduce them to the Tangi Token—the core of our solution. This transition is made easy, as the token payment option runs from the same terminal. We don't force them into token payments, but rather, we create an environment where the token becomes the obvious next step for consumers who already rely on our compliant platform. Here’s how we make the switch to token payments so attractive:

  • Incentivizing Token Use: Consumers are introduced to a range of rewards and discounts when they choose the token option. Each token purchase results in immediate benefits such as:

    • Discounts on future purchases
    • Loyalty points that can be redeemed for rewards
    • Exclusive benefits that cannot be accessed through traditional payments

This system is designed to make the token not just a payment method but a lifestyle choice for the consumer.


3. Building Engagement Through the Tangi App


To further enhance the consumer experience, we actively communicate with users through the Tangi app—a powerful tool that acts as a bridge between the company, retailers, and consumers. The app is where consumers will manage their tokens, track rewards, and stay updated on promotions and exclusive offers.

We incentivize consumers to download the Tangi app by offering them additional benefits—whether it’s a free product, early access to special promotions, or extra loyalty points. The more engaged they are, the more benefits they accrue, encouraging them to keep using the token for future transactions.


4. The Elite Rewards Program: Ensuring Token Adoption


As consumers continue using the Tangi token for payments, they become eligible for our Elite Rewards Program. This exclusive program offers tiered benefits based on the frequency of token use, creating a sense of exclusivity and ensuring long-term engagement. Consumers can earn:

  • Free products after a certain number of token-based purchases
  • Special, redeemable vouchers for use at participating retailers
  • Exclusive invitations to special offers or events
  • VIP discounts for ongoing purchases

By offering a multi-layered reward structure, we not only make token usage financially attractive for consumers, but we also reinforce the value of using the token regularly, ensuring high adoption rates and continued use over time.


5. The Benefits for Retailers


For retailers, the adoption of token payments offers several critical benefits that make the switch worthwhile:

  • Reduced Transaction Fees: The Tangi token offers retailers the most cost-effective payment solution. Traditional payment options like credit and debit cards come with high transaction fees that must be shared with third-party processors. With the Tangi token, retailers benefit from a significantly lower fee structure, allowing them to save thousands of dollars annually.
  • Increased Customer Loyalty: Retailers benefit from increased customer engagement as token users are incentivized with loyalty points and exclusive rewards. As customers use the token for payments, they’re encouraged to return, ensuring repeat business for the retailer.
  • Seamless Integration: Retailers don’t have to juggle multiple payment systems. All payment methods—traditional and token-based—are run through the same terminal, simplifying operations and streamlining the checkout process for consumers.

6. Tangi’s Profits and Company Growth


The token is not only the most rewarding option for consumers and retailers, but it’s also the most profitable for us, as the company behind it. Here’s how:

  • Increased Profit Margins: By charging a lower fee for token payments compared to traditional methods, we keep the entire fee rather than sharing it with third-party processors. This allows us to increase our revenue and profit margins as more retailers adopt the token option.
  • Scalability: With the Tangi token, we become independent of banking networks and their regulatory requirements. This gives us complete control over our ecosystem, making it easier to scale and grow the business at a much faster rate.
  • End-to-End Control: From capturing the customer to incentivizing token use and offering rewards, we control the entire value chain. This autonomy allows us to optimize every aspect of our business and deliver a consistent, high-quality experience to both retailers and consumers.

Conclusion: The Path to Widespread Adoption


In summary, our payment solution works in harmony to provide a seamless transition from traditional payments to token payments, driving consumer adoption while offering unmatched benefits to both retailers and consumers. Through our Tangi app, loyalty rewards, and cost-saving token payment option, we ensure that token adoption happens naturally, making it the preferred choice for all parties involved.

By investing in the Tangi token today, you are positioning yourself at the forefront of a groundbreaking solution that will shape the future of cannabis payments. As token adoption continues to grow, we are poised to dominate the market and offer long-term value to both investors and users alike.
