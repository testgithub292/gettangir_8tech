The traditional payment methods capture consumers and guide them toward the token, which is the more financially rewarding and cost-effective option for all involved.

3. The Road to Public Listing: Scaling Quickly with Targeted Market Penetration

Our approach is simple: we’re targeting 100 stores initially, and once we reach this milestone, the token will be listed publicly. We don’t view this as a difficult hurdle—it’s an achievable and rapid milestone that we will cross quickly as we target market dominance. These 100 stores will give us the critical mass needed to build momentum and demonstrate to investors and the broader market that we are a force to be reckoned with in the cannabis payments space.

We already have an existing user base in place with consumers who have adopted the token as their preferred payment method. By the time the token publicly lists, it will already be in mass use across these initial 100 stores. The token adoption will be well underway, with consumers actively using it for payments and reaping the benefits, and retailers eagerly promoting the token to drive customer loyalty.

This creates a huge advantage for the token at the time of listing: demand will already be there. The token will not be listed with just speculation behind it; instead, the value will be driven by the real-world use it has already seen.
